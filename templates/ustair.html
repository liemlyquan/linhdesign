ustair

The aim of the project with the slogan “do exercise whenever you can” is to encourage people to use stairs instead of elevators and escalators. Every movement can count and make joyful footprints on stairs due to interactive images and sound effects. A friendly design with neon colors and an unique footprints for every steps.

Collaborative project with Epoch Team
